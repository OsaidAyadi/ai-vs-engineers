What AI is Really Good At

  • ⚡ Boilerplate Code: Writing components fast.
  • 🔄 Repetitive Tasks: The work you hate doing.
  • 📖 Explaining: Reading old, messy legacy code.
  • 🧪 Unit Tests: Writing test cases in seconds.
  • 📝 Documentation: Writing the comments you always skip.
AI is like a very fast, very blind intern. Useful. Needs supervision.

What AI is Terrible At

  • ❌ Business Logic: It doesn't know your client or their needs.
  • ❌ Big Picture: Fixes a button, accidentally breaks the DB.
  • ❌ Accountability: AI can't go to jail. You can. 👮
  • ❌ Context: Hallucinates APIs that don't exist.
  • ❌ Judgment: It thinks every app needs Kubernetes. 😭

Code Review: Never Trust AI Blindly!

You are the last line of defense. Don't fall asleep on the job. 💂

When you copy-paste AI code without reading it...

= Technical Debt 💸

✅ Always ask yourself:

  • ❓ Do I understand what this code does?
  • ❓ Is there a simpler way to do this?
  • ❓ Could this blow up in production?
  • ❓ Is there a security or privacy risk?
The rule: If you can't explain it to your teammate, don't merge it. Ever.
