The Fear Is Real

Let's be honest. AI can now:

  • Write 500 lines of code in 10 seconds
  • Fix bugs (sometimes)
  • Talk like a human

It is normal to feel scared. But the real story is very different.

What AI is Very Good At

  • Boring Code: Making components fast.
  • Repeating Tasks: Doing the things you hate.
  • Explaining: Reading old, messy code.

AI is just a very fast, very blind cat.

What AI is Very Bad At

AI when production goes down.

  • Business Logic: AI doesn't know what your client actually wants.
  • Big Picture: It breaks the database while fixing the button.
  • Taking the Blame: AI cannot go to jail for a data leak. You do.

Clean Code is More Important Now

AI writes code that "works", but it is usually very messy and hard to read.

  • You must clean the AI code.
  • Your team needs to read it easily.
